Help & guide

How to use
the app?

Everything you need to get started. No tutorials — the app is simple by design.

🧾

Managing bills

Add bills manually or scan QR codes from invoices. Mark them as paid, edit or archive. Each bill can have a recurrence set — monthly, quarterly or yearly.

📷

QR & barcode scanner

The app recognises ZBP format from Polish invoices and standard utility barcodes (electricity, gas). Scanning automatically fills in the name, amount and payment details — no manual entry.

📈

Trends & statistics

In bill details you'll find a payment trend chart and a badge with percentage change (e.g. +7% ↑). The Statistics tab shows monthly expenses, category breakdown and comparison with the previous month.

🔔

Notifications

The app reminds you about upcoming payments 3 days before the due date. In settings you can enable an additional reminder on the day of payment at 9:00 AM. Notifications work without internet.

💾

Backup

Your data is stored locally only. In Settings → Backup you can export the database file (.db) and share it via email or Drive. To restore: select the file and confirm.

🔒

Privacy & offline mode

Twoje Rachunki doesn't require an account or internet connection (except for displaying ads). Your financial data never leaves your device — no servers, no telemetry, no registration.

💡

Tip: When you open the QR scanner for the first time, the app will show a one-time tip about supported code formats. You'll find them on most utility and service invoices.
