FAQ

Questions & answers

Do I need any marketing experience?
No. Postomat is built for small business owners with no time or marketing knowledge. Just enter a topic — AI handles the rest.
How good is the post quality?
AI is tuned for your industry and language. Posts sound natural and on-brand. Not happy? Tap "Generate again".
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your history is stored in Firebase and only you can access it. No data is shared with third parties.
Does the app post for me automatically?
No. You copy and paste yourself — full control over what gets published.
What happens after 5 generations?
The counter resets at midnight. Premium removes the limit entirely for €3.99/month.
Can I cancel Premium?
Yes, via Google Play Store. You keep access until the end of the billing period.
